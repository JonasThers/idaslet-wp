Idaslet

Idaslet is a danish folk metal band based in Copenhagen

Idaslet sprouts in the soil of the Danish underground. With their version of folk metal, Idaslet set off on their first viking voyage in 2020 with the release of their EP "Lurernes Glammen", and has now set sail for the Danish cities. With roots firmly planted in sagas and folk songs, Idaslet sings Danish stanzas both with growl and beautiful clean vocals.

Accordions and guitars enrich the soundscape with folk melodies, while drums and bass deliver rhythms that invite you to dance and sing along.
